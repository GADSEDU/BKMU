ACTIVITIES AT THE CENTRAL ZONE

Meetings

The Ahliyya committee members hold monthly meeting as well as emergency meeting when the need arises. We also have plan to start a quarterly general meeting with zonal members.

Islamiyyah

The zone has the following categories of Islamiyyaat:

  • Children Islamiyya section
  • Women Islamiyya section
  • Tahfeez (Quran Memorization) section
  • Adult Class

Mosque Lessons

  • Friday Lesson (Tawheed)
  • Saturday Lesson
  • Sunday Lesson (Fiqh)
  • Sunday After Subhi 20 mins reminder
  • Monthly Daâ€™wah general Lecture

Workshop for Teenagers

The Zone conducts Workshop for Teenagers at end of every Term for moral upbringing

Visitations

The Zone organizes various visitations as follows

  • Routine Visitation to members that are inactive
  • Visitation to Sick members
  • Condolence Visitation
  • Reconciliation Visitation/Intervention (Sulh)

Ramadan Feeding Program

The zone with the collaboration of the RA Zonal Welfare Committee distributes food items during Ramadan to zonal members.

Walimah

The zone conducts the following categories of Walimah

  • Children Walimah
  • Women/Children Walimah
  • General Zonal Walimah
  • General Ummah Walimah

Contribution/Sources of Income

All Ahliyya members are to contribute monthly pledges as deem fit. All zonal members also contribute monthly in cash or kind to support zonal and general ummah activities.
